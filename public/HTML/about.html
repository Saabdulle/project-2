About Florin Council

Our Aim

The Florin County Council is facing budget cuts and is seeking a solutions to address a problem faced by the local community. The council recognizes the importance of community involvement to sustain valued public services. The community members are concerned about the closure of public services, overflowing recycling centers, loss of local knowledge and skills, and degradation of the local landscape. The aim of the council is to work together with the community to ensure the continuation of important public services. The council would like the help of the community to be part of the solution to some of the issues the community is facing through our new volunteering initiatives.
